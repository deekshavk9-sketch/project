About VCET Alumni Nexus

The Alumni Nexus is dedicated to strengthening connections between graduates and current students — facilitating mentorship, jobs, and lifelong relationships.

Mission

To create a supportive and active alumni community that gives back to students and fosters professional networking.

What we offer

  • Alumni directory and member profiles
  • Event calendar, meetups and webinars
  • Job postings and referral board
  • Mentorship & guest lectures
